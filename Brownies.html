Brownie

A brownie is a popular chocolate dessert known for its rich, fudgy texture and deep cocoa flavor. Made from simple ingredients like chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour, brownies can range from cakey to gooey depending on the recipe. Often enjoyed plain or with added nuts, chocolate chips, or a dusting of powdered sugar, brownies are a beloved treat for their perfect balance of sweetness and indulgence. Whether served as a snack, dessert, or at celebrations, brownies remain a classic favorite among chocolate lovers worldwide.
