Cold Coffee

Cold coffee is a refreshing and popular beverage made by blending chilled coffee with milk, ice, and often a touch of sugar or flavored syrup. It’s smooth, creamy, and perfect for cooling down on hot days while providing a delightful caffeine boost. Sometimes topped with whipped cream or chocolate syrup, cold coffee offers a sweet and energizing treat enjoyed worldwide. Whether homemade or served at cafés, it’s a favorite drink for coffee lovers who want a tasty and invigorating way to stay refreshed.
