Icecream

Ice cream is a popular frozen dessert made from cream, milk, sugar, and flavorings, churned to create a smooth and creamy texture. Available in a wide variety of flavors, from classic vanilla and chocolate to fruity and exotic blends, ice cream is loved for its sweet, refreshing taste and cool sensation. It can be enjoyed in cones, cups, or as part of elaborate sundaes and desserts. Perfect for hot weather or any time a sweet treat is desired, ice cream remains one of the most cherished and universally enjoyed desserts around the world.
